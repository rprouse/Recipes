# The only White Sauce Pasta Recipe you'll ever need!
An extraordinary pasta in creamy white sauce that is simple to make and delicious to eat.

## White Sauce Pasta

### Ingredients
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 3 pieces of garlic, finely chopped
- 1 teaspoon avocado oil
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Fresh cracked pepper, to taste
- ½ cup cashews
- 2 cups water
- ¼ pound pasta (linguine or your choice)
- ¼ cup hemp hearts (optional)
- ¼ cup plant-based parmesan
- Freshly chopped parsley, for garnish

### Steps
1. Bring a pot of water to boil for the pasta.
2. In a sauté pan over medium heat, add the avocado oil, onions, and garlic. Sauté for 3 to 4 minutes.
3. Season with salt and fresh cracked pepper, then add the cashews and stir.
4. Pour in the water and bring the mixture to a boil.
5. Cook the pasta according to package instructions (about 9 minutes for linguine).
6. Transfer the onion and cashew mixture to a blender. If using, add the hemp hearts and blend on high until completely liquefied.
7. Place the sauté pan back on medium heat and pour in half of the sauce.
8. Once the pasta is cooked, transfer it to the pan with the sauce.
9. Grate in the plant-based parmesan and stir in a quarter cup of reserved pasta water to thin out the sauce.
10. Turn off the heat and stir again to combine.
11. Plate the pasta, adding more parmesan, freshly chopped parsley, and additional cracked pepper for garnish.
12. Enjoy your delicious creamy white sauce pasta!
