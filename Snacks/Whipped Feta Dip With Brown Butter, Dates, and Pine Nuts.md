# Whipped Feta Dip With Brown Butter, Dates, and Pine Nuts

This whipped feta dip is topped with brown butter–sautéed dates, toasted pine nuts, lemon, and flaky sea salt for a sweet-and-salty appetizer.

### 🛒 Ingredients
- 3 tablespoons unsalted butter (1 1/2 ounces; 43 g)
- 2 tablespoons pine nuts (1/2 ounce; 16 g)
- 8 Medjool dates (8 ounces; 227 g), pitted and torn into bite-size pieces (see notes)
- 1/8 teaspoon Diamond Crystal kosher salt; for table salt, use half as much by volume
- 1/2 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
- 1 recipe whipped feta dip
- Flaky sea salt (such as Maldon)
- Grilled or seared bread, pita chips, or crackers, for serving (see notes)

### 🥣 Steps
1. In a small skillet, melt butter over medium-low heat. Add pine nuts and cook, stirring frequently, until pine nuts and milk solids are golden, 4 to 5 minutes. Using a slotted spoon, immediately transfer nuts to a small plate to prevent further darkening, leaving butter in skillet.
2. Add dates and salt to butter in skillet. Cook, stirring occasionally, until dates soften, become glossy, and begin to slump into butter, about 3 minutes. Off heat, stir in lemon juice and reserved pine nuts.
3. Spoon warm topping over whipped feta, making sure to drizzle remaining butter in the skillet. Garnish with flaky salt, then serve with bread, pita chips, or crackers.

> [!tip] Tip
> This is the warm topping — spoon it over a base of whipped feta dip (a separate recipe), which can be made up to 3 days ahead. Tear the dates into irregular pieces for more varied texture, and pull the pine nuts from the butter the moment they're golden so they don't over-darken.
