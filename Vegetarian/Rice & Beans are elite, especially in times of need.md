# Rice & Beans are elite, especially in times of need
A comforting dish of rice and beans, perfect for budget-friendly meals or as a hearty side.

# Pinto Beans
Ingredients
- 1 lb pinto beans
- Water (for soaking and cooking)
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 piece onion
- Salt (to taste)
- Optional: Bay leaf

Steps
1. Sort through the pinto beans to remove any stones or broken beans.
2. Place the beans in a large pot and cover with water.
3. Bring the water to a boil and let the beans boil for 10 minutes.
4. Turn off the heat and let the beans soak for 1 hour.
5. Drain the soaking water and rinse the beans with cool water.
6. Fill the pot with clean water and add the beans, garlic, and onion.
7. Bring to a boil and cook for about 1 hour, adding salt about halfway through.
8. Remove any floating beans and continue to cook until tender.

# Mexican-Style Red Rice
Ingredients
- 1 cup uncooked rice
- 2 cups chicken broth (or water)
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 onion (chopped)
- Ground cumin (to taste)
- Black peppercorn (to taste)
- Optional: Garlic powder, salt, tomato flavored chicken bullion powder

Steps
1. Heat oil in a pan and toast the uncooked rice until slightly golden.
2. Add chopped onion to the pan and continue to sauté.
3. Stir in ground cumin and black peppercorn.
4. Mix in the tomato paste and let it cook for a minute.
5. Add chicken broth and adjust seasoning with chicken bullion powder (if using).
6. Increase heat to bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and cover.
7. Cook for 18-20 minutes without lifting the lid.
8. After cooking, let the rice sit for 10 minutes before fluffing with a fork.

# Salsa
Ingredients
- 2 tomatillos (halved)
- 1 Roma tomato (chopped)
- 1 onion (chopped)
- 1 jalapeno (whole or sliced as desired)
- 2 cloves garlic (with skin)
- Avocado oil (for spraying)
- Salt (to taste)

Steps
1. In a baking dish, combine tomatillos, tomato, onion, jalapeno, and garlic.
2. Spray with avocado oil.
3. Place in an air fryer until charred and cooked.
4. Blend the cooked ingredients until smooth, adding salt to taste.

### Serving Suggestion
Serve the pinto beans alongside the Mexican-style red rice, top with the salsa, and garnish with queso fresco and avocado if desired. Enjoy your delicious meal!
