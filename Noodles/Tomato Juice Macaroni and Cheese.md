## Tomato Juice Macaroni and Cheese
### 🛒 Ingredients
- 2 c. macaroni 
- 2 c. cheddar, grated 
- 1 3/4 c. tomato juice 
- 3/4 c. milk 
- 1–1 1/2 tsp salt Butter (a little, for top)
### 🥣 Steps
1. Cook macaroni & drain. 
2. Mix macaroni with cheese (save some for top). 
3. Add tomato juice, milk & salt. Stir well. 
4. Put in buttered Pyrex dish. 
5. Sprinkle rest of cheese on top. Dot with butter. 
6. Bake 350° about 40–45 min until bubbly. 
7. Let sit a few minutes.
